Excursions

"Take your mind on a journey away from the problem to find new possibilities. The ‘excursion’ is a way to get fresh, new ideas when you need them..

How to use it:

  1. Temporarily put the problem out of mind. Ex. Close your eyes.
  2. Generate irrelevant material. Ex. Facilitator talks participants through a journey to a specific place, or an imagined scenario.
  3. Improvise a novel idea. Ex. When you open your eyes, you record ideas that were inspired by the "excursion".
