Math

Duration: 30 minutes
Tools used: Forced Connections

  1. Teach the students about trading and the purpose of it.
  2. Teach the students about money.
  3. Tell the students to brainstorm for 20 minutes to figure out how they can bargain.
  4. Give students 5 objects each, and tell them to go outside and trade with other students.
  5. After the 20 minutes is up, give each student a separate checklist of things they need to have traded with other classmates. Inform them that they have 15 minutes (or until you tell them to stop).
