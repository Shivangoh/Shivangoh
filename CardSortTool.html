"Card sort is a way to rank or prioritize when you have many promising options (a minimum of six options).

How to use it:

  1. With each option on a separate card or slip of paper, mark a 6 on your least favorite, and set it aside. Of the remaining options, mark a 1 on your favorite, and set it aside. Of the remaining options, mark a 5 on your least favorite, and set it aside; continue, marking an item 2, then 4, then 3.

  2. This technique does not make a selection, but does provide a priority or rank.
