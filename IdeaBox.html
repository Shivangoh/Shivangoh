Idea Box

The Idea Box is a creativity tool for creatives and non-creatives that is designed to help people generate creative ideas with pen and paper.

How to use it:

  1. Beginning with the problem statement, select the essential characteristics of the problem, and list those on paper as column headings.
  2. For each column, list a variety of options.
  3. Then, mix and match, choosing one from each column.
